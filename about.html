About

My name is Alex Hunt. In December 2021, I graduated from Worcester Polytechnic Institute with a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science & Interactive Media and Game Development.

My goal is to pursue a career in programming. I've had a passion for sofrware (particularly) video games from a young age, particularly the experiences they can create for players. Games can inspire emotion in players through a variety of ways, but my absolute favorite kinds of games are ones that can capture the player's heart with compelling stories and characters, like Fallout: New Vegas, the Elder Scrolls series, and the Mass Effect series. However, I also enjoy games without an emphasis on story, like Doom Eternal!

I'm skilled in Unreal Engine 4 and Unity Engine, having completed projects in both. I am also experienced in software development using JavaScript, C++, and Java.

I'm currently looking for a full-time job, so feel free to contact me with any opportunities that you believe would be a good fit!
